La La Trip

2019.12.09

The movie of La La Land explores the joy and pain of pursuing their dreams in Los Angeles, CA. I watched this movie last year. It was a great story even though they couldn’t be together at the end but the girl fulfilled her dream in Hollywood.

I haven’t visited La La Land for couple of years so we flew to LAX at midnight of 4/12/2019. It was poppy flower season. This winter, California had lot of rains so the poppy flowers got blossom.

Dense carpets of orange poppies have bloomed along the hillsides and ridges at the Antelope Valley California Poppy Reserve in Lancaster, CA. The golden poppies are at peak and likely will last through the end of April. I was there at the right time and there were so many people over there. It has a 8-miles hiking trail.

Poppy flower is California’s state flower. They have a short life. They will die after flowers gone but seeds are used for food and medicine.

To best see the poppies, time your visit on a sunny or light-filled day because flowers close up when it’s overcast. They close up at night as well as on cold, windy days. Lucky for us, it was a perfect Saturday with sunshine. We got up early and drove on the road at 7:30am. We arrived there after one and half hours driving. The state park was full already. We had to park our car on the side of the road and then walked into the park.

Southern California’s unusually plentiful winter showers have brought spring flowers in abundance, with millions of tiny bright blooms popping up almost overnight and blanketing the area in a rare phenomenon known as a “super bloom.”

After 3 hours playing with poppy flowers, we headed to a Chinese restaurant that is 1.5 hours away from poppy field and another 1.5 hours away from LAX airport. Di Tai Feng is a international franchise restaurant. We had dim sum in Tai Wai and the steamed buns were so delicious. Therefore, I wanted to eat them again but the flavor was not same as before. Maybe I had a high expectation. Maybe my taste had changed.

You can see how they hand-made buns

The restaurant is located in a Shopping mall. There were so many Asian restaurants in the mall. I stopped by at MeetFresh for a Tai Wan style dessert.

Bottom is shaved ice.

Japanese style cheese cake

All food looks very attractive to me. Wish I could taste them all. After late lunch, we did little shopping in the Mall. Then we decided to head back to the airport.

Our flight was a red-eye flight and we arrived at the airport at about 6pm. So we went to United Club at Terminal 7, LAX.
