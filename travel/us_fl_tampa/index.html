This Winter, Choose Summer - Honeymoon Island, FL

2020.01.11

When Chicago had winterstorms in January, I escaped to Tampa, FL for the sunny and warm weather. Tampa is a city on Tampa Bay, along Florida’s Gulf Coast. There are so many beaches in Clearwater area and my mood was to be a sun-seeker on a white sand beach.

Honeymoon Island This island formally known as Hog Island, Honeymoon Island acquired its current name when a New Yorker purchased the island in 1939 and built bungalows for honeymooners.

This island is a barrier island on the gulf coasts of Florida. The beautiful undeveloped beaches makes it a favorite for both visitors and locals. We arrived there at about 10:00am 1/11/2020. We walked along the beach for 5.6 miles until reached the tip of the beach. Along the gorgeous beach we picked up our best shells on the sand.

Olde Bay Cafe

For lunch, we headed to the Olde Bay Cafe in Dunedin, FL. The rustic fixture on the marina featuring seafood staples & draft beers, plus daily catches to go.

We sitted by an outdoor table and were able to see all kinds of ships while we were eating the fresh caught seafood.

Given my relatively healthy lunch choice, I decided I deserved a dessert (I always can find a way to convince myself that I deserve a dessert) so we stopped by at Starchans Homemade Ice Cream store and I had a blackberry sundae to satisfy my sugar craving.

Clearwater Beach

After a little while in Dunedin town, we drove to the Clearwater Beach to watch the street live show that was played by a father and son who are also players on HBO TV channel. After the show, we walked on the Pier 60 which is famous for its sunset celebrations. When the sun began sets, we went to the beach. Someone describes this white sand is as sugar-like. It is so soft.

Everything looks like so much in Tampa! It is a great escape destination for winter!
