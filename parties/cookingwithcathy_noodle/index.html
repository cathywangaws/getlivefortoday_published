2019.12.12

Cooking with Cathy – episode 3.

Today is also the last day for Game of Thrones. We are all fan of it so I got together to cook first and then watch the finale together at my basement.

The ending of Game of Thrones made people upset. However, I think that is what it is. No matter what kind of ending is, people will always not satisfied. For me, I think, the story is not ending yet. They can still develop new stories to make it continue if they want.

Cooking with Cathy makes everyone in the group to participate. Dan made mojito drink for us. Xiaoxi made carb-free noodle for us.

Andy and I made the most of work – Chinese noodle. We made two kind of sauces for noodle – soybean paste sauce and sesame paste sauce. They like them very much. They even took over the left over sauces home. We are getting ready for cooking with Cathy!
