Peng bought a case of frozen crab legs from Costco.

We all like high protein food, espically protein from seafood. We don’t like go to restaurants for getting together. We like enjoy parties at home so we can relax. Recently crab legs are becoming a popular party dishes. I don’t mind to prepare and cook for my friends.

Host a seafood boil

Ingredients:

lemons, corn on the cob, andouille sausage, seafood (crab legs, shrimps, crawfish), small red potatoes

Crab Leg Salad

Ingredients:

crab leg meat, mayonnaise, celery, paprika, dill, salt, black pepper

Instructions:

In a large bowl add all the ingredients together gently, stirring until well coated.

Refrigerate for an hour before serving
