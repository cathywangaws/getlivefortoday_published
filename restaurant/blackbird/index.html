Blackbird

2020.02.09

Address: 619 W Randolph St, Chicago, IL 60661

Tried out blackbird for restaurant week and first impression was it was a lot trendier than it appears from the outside! Came by with friends for Restaurant Week lunch.

It’s a very nice and elegant place. There’s an open kitchen in the back…and a bar immediately to the right…behind the hostess stand. They definitely make the most of their small-ish space, intimate yet not exactly on top of the people next to you. It can be a bit of a tight squeeze.

It’s nice and bright in there with clean, open vibes. It’s trendy and modern feeling as well. The staff is fantastic. The service was great. And the food was delicious.

Chef-owner Paul Kahan’s sleek flagship known for inventive fare & a popular fixed-price lunch menu. Blackbird’s considered one of Chicago’s most iconic restaurants and furnishes its very own Michelin star.
