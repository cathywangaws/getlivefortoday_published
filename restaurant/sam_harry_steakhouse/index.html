Sam & Harry’s Steakhouse

2019.12.12

Sam & Harry’s Steakhouse Sam_Harry_Steakhouse 2019-01-27

Upscale, contemporary steakhouse serving surf ‘n’ turf classics in the Renaissance Schaumburg Hotel. Address: 1551 Thoreau Dr N, Schaumburg, IL 60173

Sam & Harry’s is about savoring the high life. Enjoy innovative cuisine, prime steaks, and fine seafood in an upscale, inviting atmosphere. Our inspired contemporary American menu celebrates thoughtful preparations of the freshest ingredients from local farms, and our Award-Winning wine program includes 250 wines by the bottle and over 50 by the glass. Whether you’re joining us for cocktails, a celebratory meal, a business lunch or leisurely dinner, our team looks forward to welcoming you.

In addition to the intimate setting of the main dining room, Sam & Harry’s also offers two exquisite and unique dining entertainment experiences; The Private Dining Room, enclosed in glass, and The Terrace, our seasonal outdoor patio. Join us Wednesdays-Fridays in the summer for live music from Chicago artists
