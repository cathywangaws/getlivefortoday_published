Hana_Ramen

2020.02.23

Located in: McCarran Marketplace (Near LAS airport)

Address: 5715 S Eastern Ave #104, Las Vegas, NV 89119

Hana Ramen is a Yokohama-style Japanese ramen restaurant in Las Vegas. It is the only restaurant that serves authentic pork back fat ramen in Las Vegas. Their homemade broth is cooked for over 16 hours and everything is made from scratch guaranteeing you the freshest and most authentic Japanese ramen.

Miso Ramen

$10.95 Pork and chicken broth served with miso dare, spicy miso, ground pork, bean sprouts, cabbage (thick noodle)

Meat Lover Ramen

$13.95 Shoyu dare, pork belly, spicy miso, ground pork, chunks of pork back fat in pork and chicken broth, egg, black garlic (thin noodle)

The grilled pork is very tasty. The broth is really rich. Must gain few pounds after a bowl of Ramen!
