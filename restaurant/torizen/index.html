Torizen, Schaumburg IL

2019.12.18

Address: 22 E Golf Rd, Schaumburg, IL 60173 Modest strip-mall eatery offering heaping bowls of ramen noodles plus other Japanese dishes.

This place is a real hidden gem. TL’s Four Seasons is an excellent Chinese and Japanese restaurant. Their sushi is excellent too.

The staff is very polite and friendly. The food is very high quality, with very generous portions and reasonable prices.

Overall, I recommend this place to everyone.
