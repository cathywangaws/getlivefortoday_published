McKays Fish & Chip Shop, Pitlochry, Scotland

2019.12.18

McKays Fish & Chip Shop, Pitlochry, Scotland

Address: 142 Atholl Rd, Pitlochry PH16 5AG, UK

The fresh North Sea haddock is very popular.

This property is located on the historical General Wade’s Road constructed during the Jacobite rebellions of the 18th century. It was built as part of King George I’s attempt to repress the Highlands, General Wade constructed a new military road ‘for wheel carriages’ between Dunkeld and Inverness.

It was a very popular place for a meal so the waiting time was about 20 mins. We didn’t have to eat over there so we asked to-go box.

The Fish was fresh with the batter being crispy & the chips easily some of the best I have had.
