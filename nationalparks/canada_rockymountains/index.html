THE WORLD AT YOUR FEET

Andy and I like adventures. We like to explore new places. We like to try new food. We like new scenic drives. As a world heritage site, Banff National Park offers rugged beauty, unspoiled landscapes and pristine mountain ranges.

Our first visit was at October 15, 2010. At that time, the summer was over and the fall season began. We had to wear our down coats but the scenery was amazing. It had light snow, frozen ice, and colorful meadows. We got inspired by the Rockies.

After 3 years later, we went back to Canadian Rockies for 9 days vacation. We decided to hike on trails this time.
