10 ways to have a better conversation

  1. No multi-task (Put away your phone / Focus on conversation)
  2. No lecture participate (Don’t change sb’s mind / Better listener / Everyone you’ll meet, you will learn!)
  3. Open questions (Ask simple questions)
  4. Go with flow
  5. Say “No” if you don’t know
  6. Don’t equate your experience with others (Just listening, don’t say anything / They just need witness)
  7. Don’t repeat yourself (If you repeat, you are literally training sb to stop listening to you)
  8. Don’t waste sb’s attention
  9. Listen (Hardest / Listen has to be learned)
  10. Be brief (Say one thing at a time)
