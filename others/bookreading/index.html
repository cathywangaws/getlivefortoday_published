I am still not used to use onenote or word document to keep track my daily progress. I have difficulty to find my previous notes in a e-file document. Here is little summary that I learned from the Bullet Journal video.

Bullet Journal is organized in cohesive. First of all, you need a note book. There are 3 major components in a bullet journal ‚Äì symbols, scheduling, indexing.

At the end of each day, go through every bullet. Put cross in the circle that presents it is done. Strike line presents the event/task is not happened. Put > presents it is rescheduled, etc. To make sure nothing is forgotten.

Symbols (task-square, event-circle, Note-dash, Rescheduled to a later date-Greater than(>), Postponed but date TBD-less than(<))

Indexing: project log, daily log, ideas etc that is at first page in your journal book.
