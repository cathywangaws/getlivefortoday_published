It is almost end of the year again. Time flies and I need to pause here and recall what have I done during year 2019.

Year 2019

  • Starting reading books

    • This is becoming a new hobby to me. I met a new friend who introduced me to join a reading club on WeChat. We were supposed to read a book each month. We just need to read 25 mins a day but we need to write down a summary after reading each day. This is really help me to remember what I read. Summary is a big benefit about understanding a book.
    • Then I also joined company’s uRead club. We read a book every quarter and then we meet for 90 mins to discuss it but related with work which is also interesting. However, I feel the discussion is deep for me. I like to attend it and learn sth from colleagues.

  • Starting writing travel journeys

    • Since my mom needed to go back to China and we didn’t know when so we had to keep our vacation for her. It means we can’t travel too much this year. So I started to look back my travel pictures and write about my trips and my feelings.
    • There is also a good opportunity on WeChat. A Chinese group hosted a Travel account on WeChat and they post articles who are interested in travel subject. This encourage me to keep writing sth every week.

  • Went to China few times

    • I usually don’t go to China often because it is a long trip without business seats. However, this year is different. I had to make few trips over there.
    • At the end of May, went back to my hometown to look for a good senior house to live for my mom.
    • At the end of October, checked in the senior house with my mom.
    • At the end of November, carried my mom’s clothes luggages back to China.

  • Starting investment by myself

    • We have a financial advisor but we just met him two times a year and we realized that the commision fee is really high. So we started to think why can’t we manage our finance by ourself.
    • Started reading dummy books
    • Opened a new trading account for us and plan to invest by ourselves. We go conservative and low commision cost.

  • Learning new technology - AWS S3 Web Hosting

    • I have had my personal blog for couple of years. I don’t have readers but myself. I do it just for fun and keep my memory refreshed.
    • Cloud is a new technology in Information industry. So I started thinking to build my blog by using AWS S3 bucket from scratch. In this way, I can learn new technology and also get hands-on experiences.

  • Passed the AWS Practitioner Exam
