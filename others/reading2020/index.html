Reading 2020

2020.01.04

It is time to take reading to the next level

This year is a decade year and I am going to focus on finance books first so I borrowed these books from the library at 1/3/2020.

Rich Dad Poor Dad

My first book I have read on financial literacy! It is a life-changing book for me. Now I have desire, passion, and interest to invest in learning about finance. My goal is to invest money by myself instead of a financial advisor. I believe it is never too late to start investment by myself!

  • The rich don’t work for money
  • Most people only talk and dream of getting rich. We need action.
  • Opportunities come and go. Being able to know when to make quick decisions is an important skill!
  • True learning takes energy, passion, and a burning desire.
    • Financial literacy
    • Understanding the market
    • Train your mind and courage and practice. Remember to have fun.
    • Don’t be afraid of losing, because failure is part of the process of success.
    • Work to learn. Don’t work for money!
