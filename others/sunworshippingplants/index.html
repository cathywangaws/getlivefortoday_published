Sun-Worshipping Plants

2019.12.09

Barrington Hills Farm Address: 2790 Spring Creek Road, Barrington Hills, IL 60010

My friend posts some sunflower pictures on WeChat Moments. So I was curious where she went for taking those beautiful sunflower pictures. Surprisingly, it is in Barrington Hills, IL. I was so excited and invited my other friends to go together for photo shooting.

Usually, the best time for taking picture is either morning or sunset time. We went over there at Sunday late afternoon. The sky was little cloudy all day long so the golden color didn’t come out in this field. We used Nikon and Cannon cameras with manual settings so the pictures were turned out not bad at all.
