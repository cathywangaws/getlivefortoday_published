The Temple of the Sea - Monaco

2017.11.17

The Principality of Monaco is about 15km (9.3 miles) from the state border with Italy. The city is a resort for tourists and a tax haven for businesses. It is a small country so you can visit it in few hours because the whole state is not larger than 2 square kilometers which is half size of New York’s Central Park. We played by ear in this small charm country at Nov 17, 2017.

When dusk falls upon Côte d’Azur, the famous Monaco splendour comes to life. Sports cars ride along narrow streets, luxurious yachts are moored in port. But in the daytime this city-state looks completely different. After we parked our car in a basement parking garage, we started walked around the port. There were so many exclusive yachts here. I have heard that the desire of possession is more pleasurable than possession itself. If so, this is just perfect place to savour with those luxurious boats!

Monaco is the second smallest country on eartt and levies no personal tax income, though the company registration is very difficult. The unemployment rate is at 0% with about 50,000 workers who commute everyday from France and Italy, and the poverty rate is the lowest in the world. Monaco has the highest number of millionaires and billionaires in the world. Would you like to live here? No problem! In 2011 a square meter of real estates costed about $65,600. Happy saving till the end of the world, hehehe.

Monte Carlo casino is marble paved “atrium”, surrounded with 28 Ionic columns made of onyx, gives access to the Opera Hall (no visits) which is entirely decorated in red and gold, with bas-reliefs and sculptures. For more than a century, its stage has been the setting for international operatic creations, prestigious concerts, and exceptional ballets. Its series of “Gaming Rooms” are marvellously decorated with stained glass windows, sculptures, and allegorical paintings. All of these add a feeling of old world elegance to the dripping-on-money allure!

Monaco’s flag is the same as the flag of Indonesia. The only difference is that the Indonesian flag is wider. The red and white colors of the flag are the heraldic colors of the Grimaldi House.

There have been three James Bond films which feature the casino. Never Say Never Again, Golden Eye, and Casino Royale. Roger Moore, a previous James Bond has a house there.

Monaco Ville is above the harbour on the rocky promontory lies a historical part which is the official capital of the country. We strolled around the narrow alleyways of Old Town Monaco and sipped a cup of coffee in this medieval town.

  • The local people of Monaco are called Monegasque. A person born in a foreign country but who is a resident in Monaco is a Monacoian.
  • Monaco has the largest police force in the world. It is not a dangerous country, but the people who visit and live there, are so wealthy and powerful that they need protection.

Monaco may be a playground for the rich and famous but we don’t have to be wealthy to enjoy this principality!
