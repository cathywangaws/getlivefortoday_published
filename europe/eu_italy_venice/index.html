Venice charms, captivates and seduces with its dazzling and unique beauty, encapsulated in its enchanting buildings and picturesque waterways.

Gondola

This was the highlight in Venice, Italy. It is a must to do in Venice. We shared the Gondola with another two people. They were boyfriend and girlfriend relationship. The boy came from Argentina and the girl come from London to meet at Venice.

Gondola service is everywhere in Venice and they only accept cash.

The sky was getting dark. The Gondola service was closed.

This group of people was singing in Gondola. They were Chinese so they song in Chinese.

Italian Food

The Venetian Streetfood. The taste was better than a restaurant dish:)

Gelato is ice cream made in the Italian style. Gelato is simply the Italian word for ice cream, but in English, it has come to mean specifically Italian or Italian-style ice cream. Gelato is made with a base of milk and sugar. It is generally lower in fat than other styles of ice cream.
