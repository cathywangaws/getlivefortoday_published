Tower Bridge - London, United Kingdom

2019.12.09

Tower Bridge is a combined bascule and suspension bridge in London, built between 1886 and 1894. The bridge crosses the River Thames close to the Tower of London and has become an iconic symbol of London. Because of this, Tower Bridge is sometimes confused with London Bridge, situated some 0.5 mi (0.80 km) upstream.

Tower Bridge Exhibition can take you to the top of the bridge, equipped with a glass floor, as well as to the bottom to the bridge’s engine rooms. I am not fan of glass floors on the top of the bridge so I didn’t go! I walked on the bridge deck from one end to another end. It was a beautiful early sunny morning so there weren’t a lot of people on the bridge yet.
