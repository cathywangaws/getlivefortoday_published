Pomegranate mojito

Happy Christmas Eve!! I can’t believe today is the last post in the year 2019. Today I am going to finish with a nice, celebratory festive cocktail.

This pomegranate mojito is an easy twist on a classic that tastes more complex than it is. With a delicious tartness, bright color and being so easy to make, it is perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 10 mint leaves
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1⁄2 lime
  • 1 1⁄2 oz white rum 45 ml
  • 3 oz pomegranate juice 90 ml
  • 2 oz sparkling water 60 ml

Instructions * Cut the lime half into 4 wedges * Place the lime wedges, mint leaves and sugar in the bottome of a glass and muddle them together * Top up the glass with ice then pour over the rum * Add the pomegranate juice then topu up the glass with sparkling water * Stir gently to mix

Notes * If you want to make this as a pitcher, making simple syrup ahead of time rather than using sugar (1⁄2 cup sugar + 1⁄2 wter in a small pan and stir to dissovle the sugar). You can add mint leaves to infuse in there as well. * Then use juice of 4 limes, the simple syrup and some crushed mint leaves along with 12oz rum. * Add 1 1⁄2 pints (720ml) pomegranate juice. * Divide between 8 glasses filled with ice, and top up with sparkling water.

Cranberry cocktail

Every year, I host a New Year Eve party for my friends and I always like to serve something new and different. I am thrilled with how much my friends have all enjoyed it!

What better way to end of my working year, than with a nice cocktail? This gorgeous, ruby drink is based on gin and cranberry juice, with a little lemon juice in there, just for a little hint of something zingy.

It’s the perfect way to finish the Christmas Season on a high, and I’ll be enjoying a glass (or two) in the party!

Ingredients

  • 1 measure Gin (2 if you like)
  • 150 ml Cranberry Juice
  • 10 ml Lemon Juice
  • Ice Cubes
  • Fresh Cranberries
  • Mint Leaves

Instructions

  • Fill a tumbler glass with ice cubes and add the gin and lemon juice.
  • Top with the cranberry juice and stir.
  • Taste and adjust the quantities how you’d like.
  • Garnish with a few cranberries and mint leaves.
