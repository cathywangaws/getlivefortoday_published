Macha Cakes 2019-01-04 No Comments

Japanese style layered cake

12 layers cake

Roughly, 60 grams = 1⁄2 cup

1/19/2019 – made this cake again. The batter was too thick so it turned out with layers. Also no enough sugar so the taste wasn’t too good.

Making the cake

4 eggs ( 4 egg white, 3 egg yolk)
1/3 cup of sugar
1 tablespoon matcha powder
1/4 cup milk
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1/3 cup cake flour
1/4 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 teaspoon cream of tarter
Bake for 15 mins at 325F degree

Cake Directions

Separate 4 egg white and yolk into two containers (throw away 1 egg yolk)
In egg yolk container, add 1/3 cup sugar to mix
Continue to add 1 tbsp vegetable oil to mix
In a small bowl, mix matcha power with 1/2 cup room temperature milk
Add matcha milk into egg yolk container to mix together
Add cake flour into the above container to make batter
Beat egg white & sugar with an electric mixer (low, high, low speed) until it becomes foam
Add foam into the batter container to mix well
Bake for 15 mins at 325 F degree
Put on a cooling rack
