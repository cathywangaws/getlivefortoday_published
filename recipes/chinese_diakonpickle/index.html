Daikon Radish Pickle

2019.12.30

Learn how to make super easy and quick pickled daikon radishes! These pickles are so versatile that they can go with anything!

Directions

  • In a mixing bowl, toss sliced diakon with lot of salt.
  • Cover until water is released for 2 days.
  • Drain and rinse daikon, removing as much salt as possible.

Pickle Brine:

  • White vinegar 200g
  • Sake 100g
  • Brown sugar 160g
  • Light soy saurce 40g
  • Boil above liquid
  • Pour into pickled can
  • Add Jananese plams
  • Chill for overnight
