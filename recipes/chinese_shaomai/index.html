ShaoMai

2019.12.31

Shumai or Shaomai or 烧卖 is a type of traditional Chinese dumpling.

Dumpling wraps from a Chinese grocery store.

Ground pork, ginger, chicken broth, soy sauce, chives, and shrimps.

Steam for 18 to 20 mins.
