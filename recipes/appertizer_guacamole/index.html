Appertizer - Guacamole

2019.12.08

Guacamole is an avocado-based dip, spread, or salad first developed by the Aztecs in present-day Mexico. In addition to its use in modern Mexican cuisine, it has become part of international and American cuisine as a dip, condiment and salad ingredient.

It is easy to make, naturally gluten-free and vegan, and always the hit of a party.

Note: Avocados must be riped.

Of course, there are a million ways to use put this guacamole recipe to delicious use as well. It’s perfect for spreading on toast and burgers, topping your tacos and nachos, loading up your baked potatoes and deviled eggs, filling your wraps, and serving alongside any number of Mexican dishes. But about 99% of the time, I just serve it up with a big bowl of tortilla chips.
