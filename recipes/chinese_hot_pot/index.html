Homemade Hot Pepper Sauce

Ingredients: Chinese pepper Cloves (ding xiang) cao-guo star anise bay leaf ginger green onion ground hot pepper sesame seeds

Vegetable oil, ginger, green onion until bubble, take out ginger and green onion Add other ingredients, mix well, cook for 30 mins on medium heat
