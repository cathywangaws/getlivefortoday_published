Stew Beef Tendon 2018-11-18 No Comments

The beef tendons are used as an ingredient in some Asian cuisines. Tendon is tough and fibrous, but becomes soft after a long period of cooking. It contains large amounts of collagen and after boiling or stewing, it is sometimes described as mimicking the mouthfeel of high-fat cuts of beef despite its low fat content.

Cook beef tendon in a pressure cooker for 90 mins with cooking wine, soy sauce, green onion, giner etc

In a clay pot, add water, beef tendon, mushroom, napa or bean curd to cook until water is gone
