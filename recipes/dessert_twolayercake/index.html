Two Layer Cake

2019.12.08

Ingredients:

10 eggs 2 x half cup of sugar 2 tablespoon of vegetable oil 1⁄2 cup of milk 1 cup of Swans cake flour 1⁄2 teaspoon of cream of tarter 1⁄2 teaspoon baking powder Wax paper Cooking racks Icing knife

Frosting:

3 cups of heavy cream 1⁄4 cup confectioners sugar 1⁄2 teaspoon vanilla Beat cream until it begins to thicken; add sugar gradually, add vanilla, Until cream holds its shape when the beater is raised

Directions:

  1. Mix one cup of cake flour, half cup of sugar and half teaspoon of backing powder together to make dry ingredient

  2. Separate 10 eggs into two bowls, one is for egg white, and another is for egg yolk

  3. In egg white bowl, add 1⁄2 teaspoon of cream of tartar and mix them together by using high speed mixer. Meanwhile, add sugar little by little until the foam is straight up and no change of shape.

  4. Add milk, 2 tablespoon of cooking oil into egg yolk bowl, and mix a little bit to make wet ingredient

  5. Add dry ingredient into wet ingredient bowl, mix them together

  6. Add foam into wet ingredient bowl. During mixing, turn around utensil and turn around the bowl one way until the batter color is even. Using rubber spatula.

  7. Preheat oven to 325F

  8. Put batter into 2 10-inch pans (with wax paper) and bake about 20 mins

  9. Cool cakes face down on cooling rack
