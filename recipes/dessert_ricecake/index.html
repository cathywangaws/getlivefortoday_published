Sweet Rice Cake 2018-11-18 No Comments

1 bag of sweet rice flour

1⁄2 cup of sugar

3 eggs

1 1⁄4 cups of milk or more

1 ts baking powder

1⁄3 cup vegetable oil

walnuts / pecan

red bean paste

Add sweet rice flour, sugar, baking powder in a bowl and mix well

Add eggs, milk, and oil in another bowl and mix well (electric mixer)

Add flour into the second bowl to make batter

In a rectangle baking pan, spray oil, and then spread half of batter

Add a layer of red bean paste (make round ball)

Spread rest of batter on the top

Spread nuts

cover with foil to prevent nuts burned

365F oven, bake for 60 mins
