Baked Spring Roll

2019.12.30

Delicious and crunchy without deep frying!

Ingredients

  • Cabbage
  • Carrot
  • Brown mushroom
  • Spring roll wrapper (7 inch square)
  • Spring roll sauce

Directions

  • Shreded cabbage and carrot
  • Stir fry them in sesame oil, don’t over cook. Add salt

  • Stir fry sliced mushrooms with oyster sauce until soft

  • Preheat oven to 425F

  • Arrange spring rolls in a single layer on a medium baking sheet.

  • Brush with vegetable oil.

  • Bake for 20 minutes, turn after 10 minutes, until hot and lightly browned.

  • Serve with Spring dippling sauce
