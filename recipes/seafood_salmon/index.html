Salmon Flake Lettuce Wrap 2018-07-07 No Comments

Simple ingredient is not only tasty but looking good.

You just need Marinated Salmon from Costco, (little chopped celery is optional) and butterhead lettuce from a grocery store.

Defrost frozen salmon at room temperature. Microwave to cook the salmon for few minutes. Make salmon flakes by using fork and chopsticks when they are still hot or warm and remove the skin. Put flakes on the lettuce. You can serve individually or together.
